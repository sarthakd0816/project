Farm Equipments

Modern Tools That Power Snehal Farms

Mitsubishi Small Tractor

The Mitsubishi Mini Tractor is a highly versatile and compact machine designed specifically for small farms and vineyards. Its lightweight structure allows easy movement in narrow farm paths. Farmers prefer this tractor for spraying, intercultivation, trailer work, and daily tasks. It is extremely fuel-efficient and low-maintenance, making it ideal for long-term farm usage.

John Deere 45 HP Tractor

The John Deere 45 HP tractor is designed for medium to heavy agricultural operations. It offers excellent torque, traction, and fuel efficiency. Perfect for ploughing, rotavator work, transport, and heavy load tasks, this tractor is known for its long durability and smooth performance even in tough field conditions.

Rotavator

The rotavator is used to prepare fine soil before sowing. It breaks hard soil, removes weeds, and mixes nutrients evenly. This machine reduces multiple field passes, saving fuel and time, while improving soil aeration and crop growth.

Knapsack Sprayer

A knapsack sprayer is a lightweight spraying device used to apply pesticides, fungicides, and foliar nutrients. It offers great coverage with minimum wastage, making it ideal for small and medium farms.

Reversible Plough

The reversible plough is essential for primary tillage. It turns soil deeply, improves aeration, breaks hard soil layers, and brings fresh nutrients to the surface — ideal for crops like sugarcane, wheat, and maize.

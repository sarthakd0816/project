Plot 2 – Development & Growth

Snehal Farms

About Plot 2

Plot 2 is an important phase in the growth of Snehal Farms. This land was developed in the late 1990s as the farm expanded beyond its original ancestral plot.

The development of Plot 2 marked a shift toward planned farming and crop specialization.

Grape Cultivation Era

After development, Plot 2 was mainly used for grape cultivation. This period helped Snehal Farms adopt improved irrigation techniques, better crop planning, and market-oriented farming.

Grapes played a major role in strengthening the farm’s experience and technical knowledge.

Plot 2 Today

Today, Plot 2 has evolved to support modern farming practices. The land is now home to net houses that provide a controlled environment for crop growth.

Along with net house farming, sugarcane is also cultivated, making Plot 2 a balanced mix of modern infrastructure and traditional crops.

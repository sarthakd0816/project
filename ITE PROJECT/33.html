About Snehal Farms

Snehal Farms started in 1985 with a small plot. Later, we expanded our land and grew grapes. Since 2017, sugarcane has been our main crop. Today, the farm follows both traditional and modern farming techniques.Established in 1985, Snehal Farms began as a single small plot with a simple vision: to cultivate the land with integrity, dedication, and respect for nature. What started as a modest family effort soon evolved into a multi-crop, multi-generation agricultural journey shaped by hard work and continuous learning. During the early years, our focus expanded as we added more land and strengthened our foundation in traditional farming. By the year 2000, Snehal Farms entered a new phase—one defined by innovation and specialization. We committed ourselves to grape cultivation, refining our techniques, adopting better irrigation practices, and aligning with modern agricultural standards. For nearly two decades, grapes remained at the heart of our operations, helping us grow in knowledge, experience, and reputation. From 2017 onward, our journey took another forward step as Snehal Farms shifted focus to sugarcane cultivation. This change marked our move toward sustainable, large-scale farming supported by improved soil management, efficient resource use, and an unwavering commitment to quality. Today, Snehal Farms stands as a blend of tradition and progress—where decades of experience meet modern agricultural practices. With every crop we grow, we continue the legacy started in 1985: nurturing the land, supporting our community, and striving toward a future of smart, sustainable farming.

About Snehal Farms

Our Journey Through Generations

Our Story

Snehal Farms has a strong agricultural foundation rooted in traditional farming practices. Farming activities began in the 1960s on a small scale, relying heavily on rainfall, manual labor, and local agricultural knowledge passed down through generations.

In 1985, Snehal Farms was formally established with a dedicated plot of land. Early cultivation focused on understanding soil conditions, seasonal patterns, and sustainable farming methods, laying a strong foundation for long-term growth.

Our Vision

The vision of Snehal Farms is to combine traditional farming wisdom with modern agricultural techniques to achieve sustainable and productive farming.

The farm aims to conserve natural resources such as water and soil, adopt environmentally responsible practices, and continuously improve crop quality while ensuring long-term agricultural growth for future generations.

Our Journey

During the 1990s and 2000s, Snehal Farms expanded its land holdings and gradually introduced improved tools, better crop planning, and irrigation support systems. This phase marked the transition from purely traditional farming to a more organized and planned approach.

A major milestone in this journey was the cultivation of grapes. Snehal Farms successfully produced export-quality grapes by following strict quality standards, precise irrigation control, and scientific crop management practices.
